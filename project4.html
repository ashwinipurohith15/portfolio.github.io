Global Cricket Performance Analysis & Player Insights (Tableau)

Scenario/Problem: The world of cricket generates vast amounts of data, yet insights into team performance trends, player strengths/weaknesses across formats, and factors contributing to match outcomes are often hidden. This project aims to analyze a global cricket dataset to uncover key performance indicators, identify top-performing players and teams, and provide actionable insights for strategists, coaches, or fantasy league enthusiasts.

  • Data Connection & Preparation: Connecting to diverse data sources (CSV, Excel), performing data blending (e.g., matching player stats with match results), and data cleaning within Tableau (handling nulls, splitting fields, reformatting dates).
  • Calculated Fields: Creating custom metrics like Strike Rate, Economy Rate, Batting Average, Bowling Average, Win Percentage, or custom performance indices.
  • Parameters & Filters: Building interactive dashboards allowing users to filter by team, player, format (Test, ODI, T20), year range, or even specific tournament.
  • Advanced Chart Types: Utilizing various visualizations such as:
    • Line Charts: Player performance trends over time, team win/loss streaks.
    • Bar Charts: Top batsmen by runs, top bowlers by wickets, team comparisons.
    • Scatter Plots: Analyzing correlation between two metrics (e.g., Strike Rate vs. Batting Average).
    • Treemaps/Packed Bubbles: Visualizing composition of runs/wickets by different phases of the game or types of dismissal.
    • Heatmaps: Showing player performance consistency or team strengths across different conditions/opponents.
  • Dashboard Design & Interactivity: Creating intuitive and visually appealing dashboards with multiple sheets, action filters, and dynamic titles to tell a compelling data story.
  • Geospatial Analysis (if location data is available): Mapping team performance or player origins globally.

Potential Insights/Impact:

  • Identification of clutch players who perform well under pressure.
  • Analysis of team strengths and weaknesses across different formats and conditions (e.g., home vs. away).
  • Predictive indicators for match outcomes based on historical player/team performance.
  • Insights into the impact of specific strategies(e.g., aggressive batting, defensive bowling).
  • Player comparison tools for talent scouting or fantasy cricket.
  • Understanding of venue-specific performance biases.

Developed an interactive Tableau dashboard to analyze comprehensive global cricket data, transforming raw statistics into actionable performance insights. Utilized advanced calculations, dynamic parameters, and a variety of compelling visualizations (e.g., trend lines, scatter plots, interactive filters) to identify key player performance indicators, team strengths across formats, and factors influencing match outcomes. This project showcases my expertise in data blending, complex visualization, and creating user-friendly, impactful dashboards for diverse audiences."
